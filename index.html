About

My name is Carl. I'm currently a 2014 Fellow at the University of Chicago's Data Science for Social Good program. I'm going to be developing my statistics & computer science skills so that I can tackle important societal issues around poverty, health and education.

I will be writing about my experiences as a DSSG Fellow throughout the summer. Subscribe via RSS to get updates!

In the past, I've worked at:

  • Clever: A venture-backed education technology startup that bridged the gap between student data and applications. I worked as an operations analyst & Python engineer.
  • Pearson: I worked at this education behemoth as a product manager on the mobile team.
  • Udemy: A SF-based online course platform for courses aimed at professional skills. I worked on the sales team.

    • I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2013, where I majored in Statistics, while dabbling in philosophy and computer science.
